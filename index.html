1. Information We Collect

Pakistan Land Converter is designed with privacy first. We keep data collection to an absolute minimum.

What we do NOT collect
  • Your name, email, or personal identity
  • Phone number or contact details
  • Location or GPS data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Photos or files from your device
  • Login or account information
  • Payment or financial information
What is stored on your device only
  • Your conversion history (saved locally, never uploaded)
  • App preferences and settings

2. Google AdMob & Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob helps keep the app free for all users.

⚠️

Google AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy, not ours.

What AdMob may collect
  • Device advertising ID (GAID)
  • Device type, model and OS version
  • App usage and interaction data
  • IP address (used for general location)
  • Crash logs and performance data
Ad Type When Shown Frequency
Interstitial (Full Screen) Between screen visits Every 5 interactions

You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

To learn more about how Google uses data: policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers or databases.

Data Type Where Stored Uploaded?
Conversion History Device local storage Never
App Preferences Device local storage Never
Ad Data (AdMob) Google servers By Google

🔒 Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data from your device. No data remains on any server because we do not use any servers.

4. App Permissions

Pakistan Land Converter requests only the minimum permissions required to function.

Permission Purpose Required
INTERNET Load advertisements via AdMob Yes
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check connectivity to load ads when online Yes
Permissions we never request
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION or COARSE_LOCATION
  • CAMERA or RECORD_AUDIO
  • READ_CONTACTS or CALL_LOG

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times.

Your rights include
  • Delete your conversion history anytime inside the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data permanently
  • Opt out of personalized ads via device settings
  • Request information about what data AdMob holds via Google
  • Contact us with any privacy concern or complaint
How to delete your history in the app
  • Open the app → go to History tab
  • Tap the "Clear All" button in the top right
  • Confirm deletion — all history is permanently removed

8. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security features (PIN, fingerprint, encryption).

🔒 We do not transmit your personal conversion data over the internet. Your land calculations and history never leave your device through our app.

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device updated and protected with a secure lock screen.
